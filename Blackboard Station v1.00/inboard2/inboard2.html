1.3 What is gamification

The use of game elements and game design techniques in non-game contexts.
Samsung: It added elements of games in order that people engage.
Zombies RUN!: the objective is not sitting and playing, but running.
Game elements: progress bar, avatars, rewards, coins, resource collection
Game design techniques
More to games than elements: games are created for the purpose to be fun. Elements can be applied outside games, not only for business but in education and other areas.
Think like a game designer
NON-GAME CONTEXT: some objective other than success in the game.

1.4 Why study gamification

1.- An emerging business practice. Many companies already use it: Microsoft, Samsung, American Express, AliExpress, and eBay, just to name a few.
2.- Games are powerful things: attractive for people. It is highly engaging.
3.- Lesson from psychology (understand about motivation), design (structure), strategy (knowing about this may help your business bigger), technology (close to people and it could easy the use of technology; and ally).
4.- It is harder than it appears.
